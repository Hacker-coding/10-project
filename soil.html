Soil Pollution

What is Soil pollution

  • Soil pollution refers to the contamination of soil with anomalous concentrations of toxic substances. It is a serious environmental concern

Causes of soil pollution

  • Industrial waste
  • Deforestation
  • Excessive use of fertilisers and pesticides
  • Garbage pollution
